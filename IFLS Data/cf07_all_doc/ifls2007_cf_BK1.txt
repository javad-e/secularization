NOTES ABOUT BOOK 1                                                         
------------------                                                         
Book I was administered in a group interview setting to the village        
leader and/or members of his/her staff.  The composition and size of       
the group varies across communities.  The purpose of the book was to       
provide information about the basic physical, social, demographic,         
and economic characteristics of the IFLS communities.
